Leash Training
Loose Leash Walking
⏱ 10 min
📊 Intermediate
✅ Positive Reinforcement

Teach Buddy to walk calmly beside you without pulling. Golden Retrievers are eager learners — this usually clicks within 3 to 5 sessions.

Step-by-Step
1
Start in a low-distraction area
Begin indoors or in your backyard. Your dog needs to focus on you before tackling the street.
2
Hold treats at your hip
Keep high-value treats at your left hip. Your dog's nose will naturally follow your hand position.
3
Stop the moment he pulls
Freeze completely. Don't move. Wait for the leash tension to release, then reward immediately.
4
Reward loose leash moments
Every 3 to 5 steps with a relaxed leash, say "Yes!" and give a treat. Make it a celebration.
💡 Golden Retriever Tip
Goldens are highly food-motivated but easily distracted by smells. Keep sessions short — 5 to 8 minutes — and always end on a success. Let sniffing be a reward for good walking behavior.
